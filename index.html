Elements of Art workshop for Grade 4 and 5

Art is unquestionably one of the purest and highest elements in human happiness. It trains the mind through the eye and the eye through the mind. As the Sun colors the flowers, so does art colors life. To enlighten the students with Art Integration in respect with subjects, a workshop on ‘Elements of Art’ was organized for Senior Primary(Grade 4 & 5) on 4th & 5th of November,2020. Ms.Anahita Lee- an eminent Consultant in Education with her prime contribution in the field of Curriculum Design and In-house Development for Educators, an Author & Series Editor of Mulberry English Course Book (Grade 1-8) and Little Artists (Grade 1-5) illuminated the students’ creative skills. Ms.Anahita explained the five core elements of art ie. lines, shapes, colors, texture and form which can be integrated with the core subjects.
